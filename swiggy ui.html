Swiggy App UI/UX Replica

Overview

A complete UI/UX replica of the existing Swiggy food delivery app, designed in Figma. The goal was to recreate the app’s interface as closely as possible, focusing on accuracy, consistency, and maintaining the original look and feel of the Swiggy mobile experience.

Replica Screens

- Home screen with categories and banners
- Restaurant listing pages
- Product detail and menu screens
- Cart and checkout flow
- Profile and settings screens

Tools Used

- Figma (UI/UX design and prototyping)
