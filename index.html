About Me

Hi! I'm Arsalan Shaikh, a software developer currently pursuing my Master of Computer Applications (MCA) at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham University, Kochi Campus. I hold a Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) degree from Dnyanprassarak Mandal's College & Research Centre, Assagao, Goa. During my studies, I built a strong foundation in software development and design. I’m skilled in Java, Python, SQL, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and have hands-on experience with Android Studio and Firebase for mobile app development. I also use Bootstrap 5 to design responsive and user-friendly websites. In addition to coding, I have a keen interest in UI/UX design and wireframing, where I focus on creating intuitive and visually appealing interfaces that enhance the overall user experience. Throughout my academic journey, I’ve worked on projects that strengthened my problem-solving, teamwork, and project management skills. I’m familiar with software development methodologies like Agile, Waterfall, and Scrum, which help me collaborate effectively and deliver high-quality software solutions. I’m always eager to learn, innovate, and grow in the tech world. If you’d like to connect, discuss ideas, or explore opportunities — feel free to reach out!
